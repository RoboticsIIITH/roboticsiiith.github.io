### <a id="about-the-program"></a>About the Program

IIIT Hyderabad has been identified as one of the Resource Centers (RC) for the **MeitY-approved project**  
**“Capacity building for human resource development in Unmanned Aircraft System (Drone and related Technology)”**.

As part of this initiative, the **Robotics Research Center (RRC), IIIT Hyderabad** is organizing the **Sixth Master Trainer Program (MTP)** for the Technology Specific Work Theme **“Allied UAS Technologies”**, scheduled from **24th to 28th February 2026**.

The five-day program includes **theory and hands-on practical sessions** covering:

- Vision foundational models  
- CNNs, Generative Models, Diffusion Models  
- Transformers, LLMs, Vision–Language–Action models  
- Model compression and deployment  
- ROS, OpenCV  
- MAVLink and vision integration with UAVs
