### <a id="about-the-program"></a>About the Program

The Fifth Master Trainer Program for the Technology Specific Work Theme **“Allied UAS Technologies”** with a focus on **Learning, Vision and Motion Planning** was conducted from **3rd to 7th March 2025** at IIIT Hyderabad.

The five-day program covered foundations of computer vision, scientific machine learning, statistical issues in AI/ML, reinforcement learning, motion planning, and applications to UAS. Tutorial sessions focused on implementation aspects of the discussed methods.

**Key Highlights**
- Focused sessions on Learning, Vision, and Motion Planning  
- Balance of theory and hands-on tutorials  
- Delivered by researchers from academia and industry
