# 1st National Workshop  
**Systems and Intelligence for UAV**

- **Dates**: 3rd – 4th November 2023  
- **Venue**: IIIT Hyderabad Campus  
- **Status**: Event concluded — registrations are closed.

---

### <a id="about-the-program"></a>About the Program

A National 2-day workshop on Systems and Intelligence for UAV was conducted on 3rd and 4th November 2023. The workshop focused on system design, motion planning, security, and control aspects of UAVs, bringing together experts from academia and industry.

---

### <a id="program-topics"></a>Program Topics

The workshop covered the following topics:

- System design of passenger drone  
- Arka Aerospace – Research to Product  
- A brief summary of recent works on quadrotor(s) motion planning at University of Tartu  
- Autonomous multi-agent systems for collaborative interception  
- IoT-enabled Internet of Drones (IoD), its security issues and applications using blockchain technology  
- Coupling effects on the dynamics of a fixed-wing nano aerial vehicle  
- Intelligent anti-swing controller for safe human interaction in quadrotors with cable suspended payload
