# Second Two-Day Workshop  
**Systems and Intelligence for UAV**

- **Dates**: 18th – 19th December 2024  
- **Time**: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM  
- **Venue**: IIIT Hyderabad Campus  
- **Status**: Event concluded — registrations are closed.

---

### <a id="about-the-program"></a>About the Program

The Second Two-Day Workshop on Systems and Intelligence for UAV focused on recent advances in Vision Transformers, SLAM, Computer Vision applications, and Motion Planning for UAV systems.

---

### <a id="program-topics"></a>Program Topics

The two-day workshop covered:

- Transformers and Vision Transformers  
- Neural Networks – MLP and CNN  
- Geometry in Vision and SLAM  
- Image Processing Techniques and Computer Vision Applications  
- Trajectory generation with splines and trapezoidal profiles  
- Motion Planning
